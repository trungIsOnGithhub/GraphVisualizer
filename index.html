How to use?

-To create a vertex/node, click on anywhere you want your vertex/node to be placed on the field.
-Make sure the field is "unfreezed" when you click on it.
-To draw a line, for example:
+Draw a line between node 1 and node 2 with weight 9, type into the input field: "1-2-9".
+Remember that we only allow positive edge weight, undirected graph & 10 nodes only.
